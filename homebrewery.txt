## Description
The dungeon has the following features, these may include skill checks to perform certain actions.
{{descriptive
#### General Features
| Type | Detail |
|:--|:--|
| Floors | Hewn Stone (DC 10 to charge or run) |
| Walls | Hewn Stone (DC 20 to climb) |
| Temperature | Cool |
| Lighting | Shadowy (phosphorescent fungus or candles every 20 ft.) |
| Special | None |
}}
Some of the corridors marked on the map have special features detailed below.
{{descriptive
#### Corridor Features
| Type | Detail |
|:--|:--|
| a | Fire Spray: DC 15 to find, DC 15 to disable;     affects all targets within a 20 ft. cone, DC 13 save or take 2d10 fire damage |
| c | Several adventurer corpses are scattered along the corridor |
| e | A narrow shaft falls into the corridor from above |
| i | Several alcoves are cut into the walls here |
| m | Electrified Floortile: DC 20 to find, DC 15 to disable;     affects all targets within a 10 ft. square area, DC 15 save or take 2d10 lightning damage |
| n | A toppled statue lies across the corridor |
| r | Arrow Trap: DC 10 to find, DC 10 to disable;     +5 to hit against one target, 1d10 piercing damage |
| s | Skeletons hang from chains and manacles against the walls |
}}
