# The Forsaken Warrens of Illfang the Kobold Lord
:
##### The dungeon was created by dwarves as a planar gate. It was eventually abandoned due to plague, and has been attacked and abandoned many times since then.
:::
##### A randomly generated D&D 5E donjon dungeon for a party size of 4 and APL1
:::
##### Created using [Homebrewery](https://homebrewery.naturalcrit.com), [Donjon](https://donjon.bin.sh) and [donjon_to_homebrewery](https://github.com/telboy007/donjon_to_homebrewery)
