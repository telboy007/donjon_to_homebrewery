# The Black Labyrinth of the Demon Princess
:
##### The dungeon was created by dwarves as a treasure vault. It was eventually abandoned by its creators, and the dungeon has lain empty for many centuries until recently.
:::
##### A randomly generated D&D 5E donjon dungeon for a party size of 10 and APL10
:::
##### Created using [Homebrewery](https://homebrewery.naturalcrit.com), [Donjon](https://donjon.bin.sh) and [donjon_to_homebrewery](https://github.com/telboy007/donjon_to_homebrewery)
}}
