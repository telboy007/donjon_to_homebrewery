## Features
The dungeon has the following features, these may include skill checks to perform certain actions.
{{descriptive
#### General Features
| Type | Detail |
|:--|:--|
| Floors | Uneven Flagstone (Acrobatics DC 10 to charge or run) |
| Walls | Superior Masonry (Athletics DC 20 to climb) |
| Temperature | Cool |
| Lighting | Shadowy (phosphorescent fungus or candles every 20 ft.) |
| Special | None |
}}
Some of the corridors marked on the map have special features detailed below.
{{descriptive
#### Corridor Features
| Type | Detail |
|:--|:--|
| a | An iron chandelier hangs from the ceiling here |
| c | A fountain of water sits in an alcove here |
| e | Numerous pillars line the corridor |
| i | A narrow shaft descends from the corridor into a natural cavern below |
| m | A jingling sound fills the corridor |
| n | Dart Blaster (Perception DC 12 to find, Thievery DC 17 to disable, Single-shot, Close blast 3, Attack +11 vs. AC, Damage 3d6+4, 63 xp) |
| r | Withered corpses are nailed to the corridor walls |
| s | Part of the ceiling has collapsed into the corridor |
| u | A chute descends from the corridor into a natural cavern below |
| v | Skeletons hang from chains and manacles against the walls |
| w | The sound of chimes fills the corridor |
| x | The scent of ozone fills the corridor |
| z | A group of demonic faces have been carved into the walls |
}}
